---
title: "How to add a collaborator to your quest"
description: "This guide walks you through how to add another NeuroFusion user as an admin to manage your quest"
---

Research is better done in groups as opposed to silos. With NeuroFusion you have ability to add other people as administrators to help manage your quest.

Follow these steps to do so:

* Get the user's public key from [https://usefusion.ai/profile](https://usefusion.ai/profile)

  ![](/fusion_user_profile)

* Head over to your quest admin page

  * In the collaborator tab, paste their public key

![](/add_collaborator)

* Hit "Update Quest"
